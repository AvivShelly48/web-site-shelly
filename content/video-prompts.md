# VELARO Brand Video Prompts

## Video 1 — Beyond The Surface
Create a cinematic luxury brand film for VELARO, a premium architectural surface systems brand. Visual language: dark charcoal stone, bronze metallic logo, minimalist architecture, slow camera movement, soft shadows, elegant reflections, elite private villas and luxury kitchens. Start with a black screen, reveal the VELARO sail-inspired V monogram in bronze, then transition into close-ups of stone, porcelain, HPL and engineered panels. Show facades and interiors as protective architectural skins. Text overlays: VELARO, Beyond The Surface, Designed To Perform, Built To Endure. Mood: quiet power, luxury, precision, architectural confidence.

## Video 2 — Designed To Perform
Create a technical-luxury film for VELARO. Show advanced production, precision cutting, CNC details, hidden fixing systems, panel edges, dry-hang facade details and premium kitchen cladding. Combine macro shots of materials with engineering diagrams and architectural line drawings. Use dark backgrounds, bronze highlights and minimal typography. The film should feel premium, intelligent and trusted by leading developers, architects and high-end clients. Text overlays: Advanced Surface Systems, Engineering Beauty, Precision In Every Layer.

## Video 3 — The Facade That Moves Architecture Forward
Create a poetic brand film inspired by the sail. Begin with a minimal line drawing of a sail moving in the wind. Transform the sail shape into the VELARO V monogram, then into a facade panel system and luxury kitchen surfaces. Use elegant transitions, slow motion, architectural shadows, premium dark stone textures and bronze details. The message: inspired by the sail, designed to protect, define and move architecture forward. Text overlays: From Vela To Velaro, Protection, Facade, Movement, Beyond The Surface.
